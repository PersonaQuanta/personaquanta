Executive mini one-pager

PersonaQuanta is an explainable self-model for users: a fast way to translate inputs into a narrative of drivers, trade-offs, and trajectory — designed to motivate completion and action without claiming precision.

Why this works (behavioral)

  • Reduces form fatigue by turning inputs into a story users want to finish.
  • Makes uncertainty explicit, increasing trust vs. “mystery scores”.
  • Creates a safe what-if loop: users learn consequences before committing.

90-second demo flow

  1. Input: a short questionnaire (pseudonym-first).
  2. Drivers: top 3 drivers + 1 constraint, in plain language.
  3. One what-if: tweak one variable → see direction + confidence change.
  4. Export: shareable PDF/JSON summary for personal or internal use.
